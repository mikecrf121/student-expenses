The Reports Managers create an Expense Report where they add students to that Report , who will then receive an email invite & create account for them if none exists already.

* New, Accounts/ Students can now be associated with more than one report.
Can only have one Reports Manager!

Those students add their expenses in their own profile through the app.

***This does not use a typical server smtp service, but instead for development uses a fake email service.

Use this to view the emails for adding students, resetting passwords, verifying emails, etc.----Ethereal Email
Check out the config file on the server for credentials.
You may create your own account for testing.

There are 3 User Groups:
1. Admins
- Create/View/Edit All Accounts
+ Create/View/Edit Everything For Reports Managers
- Create/View/Edit All Reports & Calculate Disbursements
- Create/View/Edit All Expenses For Students
2. Reports Managers
- Create/View/Edit Reports & Calculate Disbursements
- Add Students To Reports
- Create/View/Edit Expenses For Students
3. Students
- Create/View/Edit Personal Expenses
